Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Home

Description

These homemade chocolate chip cookies are soft in the center with golden edges and plenty of melty chocolate.

The batter is easy to mix, and the cookies bake up chewy, buttery, and perfect for sharing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter with sugars until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fully combined.
  4. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt together and gradually add to the wet mixture.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips, then drop spoonfuls onto the baking sheets.
  6. Bake 10–12 minutes until edges are golden, then cool on a wire rack.
