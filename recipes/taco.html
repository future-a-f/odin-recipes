Taco Casserole Recipe

Home

Description

This hearty taco casserole brings all the flavors of a classic taco dinner into an easy baked dish.

Ground beef, cheese, beans, and spices are layered with tortillas and baked until bubbly for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F and grease a baking dish.
  2. Cook the ground beef with onion until browned, then stir in taco seasoning and tomatoes.
  3. Layer tortillas, beef mixture, beans, and cheese in the baking dish.
  4. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese.
  5. Bake for 20–25 minutes until the casserole is hot and the cheese is melted.
  6. Let rest a few minutes before serving.
