Pasta Primavera Recipe

Home

Description

This pasta primavera is a bright, easy dinner with fresh vegetables and tender pasta tossed in a light garlic and olive oil sauce.

It is perfect for a weeknight meal or to serve when guests come over, and it stays colorful and satisfying every time.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup asparagus pieces
  • 1 cup zucchini slices
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta until al dente, drain, and reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil over medium heat until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, asparagus, and zucchini; cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the vegetables, adding pasta water to loosen the sauce.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve warm with extra grated cheese if desired.
